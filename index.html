FAQ

What is Laboratory by OPSA?

The OPSA Laboratory is a research testing ground focused on exploring future implications of internet technologies and infrastructure protocols, while developing and maintaining core infrastructure software.

How can I join the laboratory program?

You can join the laboratory program by clicking on the "Join Laboratory Program" button on our homepage.

What types of projects do you incubate?

We incubate projects that focus on protocol development and projects that can benefit open society. Our accelerator program provides dedicated resources to help scale selected projects.

How can I get involved in hackathons?

You can get involved in our hackathons by checking our events page or following us on social media for announcements about upcoming events.
